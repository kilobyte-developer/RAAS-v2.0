About Us

Welcome to RASS

Founded in 2018 in the serene town of Devrukh, Ratnagiri, Rangavdhut Art Studios is where creativity meets tradition. We craft soulful paintings, intricate statues, mesmerizing murals, and elegant sculptures, each piece telling a story of artistry. Rooted in sustainability, our eco-friendly Shadoo soil Ganpati idols embody devotion and nature in perfect harmony. Every creation is a blend of passion, culture, and timeless beauty, designed to inspire and enchant.
